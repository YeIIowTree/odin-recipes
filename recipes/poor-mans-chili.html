Poor Man's Chili

Ingredients

  • Can of black beans
  • Salsa

Directions

  1. Drain the can of beans
  2. Mix beans and the amount of salsa you prefer
  3. Nuke it in your microwave
  4. Eat

Extra Info

Rambling

This meal is so cheap, and it's actually pretty good! It's super quick and easy to make you don't even need a kitchen, just a microwave. Enjoy it and credit to scooby1961 for the recipe.
