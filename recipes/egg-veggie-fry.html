Egg Veggie Fry

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Frozen or fresh spinach
  • Sun dried tomatoes
  • Olive Oil
  • Spices of choice

Directions

  1. Add eggs to a bowl and mix, use less yolks for higher protein content.
  2. Add eggs, spinach, tomatoes to pan, add spices, then cook on medium
  3. Eat
